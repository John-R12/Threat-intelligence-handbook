This phase defines the **objectives and requirements** of your Threat Intelligence program.

---

## Objectives

- Identify intelligence needs based on **business and security priorities**.  
- Define scope: assets, regions, threat actors, and types of intelligence.  
- Allocate **resources and responsibilities** to CTI analysts.

---

## Key Steps

1. Define **requirements** in consultation with SOC, IR, and executive management.  
2. Prioritize intelligence needs using **risk and business impact**.  
3. Document objectives, stakeholders, and timelines.  
4. Establish **metrics and success criteria** for the intelligence program.
